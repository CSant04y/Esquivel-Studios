About Carlos

Photography has been part of my life since I was thirteen, the moment I got my first cell phone with a camera. I'd pose my siblings, my family members, even my dogs—experimenting with angles, lighting, and capturing genuine moments. That early curiosity grew into a true passion for portrait photography.

What I love most is being part of someone's special moment. Whether it's a senior milestone, a couple's engagement, or a family celebrating together, I'm there to capture memories you'll cherish for years to come. My approach is simple: get to know you, understand what you're celebrating, and create images that feel authentic to who you are.
